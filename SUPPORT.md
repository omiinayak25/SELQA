Thanks for using **OmiinQA**. Here's how to get help.

Most questions are answered in the docs:
- [README](README.md) — overview, quick start, commands
- [Architecture](docs/ARCHITECTURE.md) · [Folder Structure](docs/FOLDER_STRUCTURE.md)
- [Developer Guide](docs/DEVELOPER_GUIDE.md) · [Execution Guide](docs/EXECUTION_GUIDE.md)

- [Troubleshooting](docs/TROUBLESHOOTING.md) · [FAQ](docs/FAQ.md)

## 💬 Questions & discussion
Open a thread in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/omiinayak25/SELQA/discussions).

## 🐛 Bugs & features
Open an issue using the templates:
- [Bug report](https://github.com/omiinayak25/SELQA/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml)
- [Feature request](https://github.com/omiinayak25/SELQA/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml)

## 🔐 Security
Do **not** open public issues for vulnerabilities. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## ⏱️ Response expectations
This is a community project maintained on a best-effort basis. Please include a
minimal reproduction, your environment, and relevant logs to speed up triage.
